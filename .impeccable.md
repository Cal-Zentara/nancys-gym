## Design Context

### Users
Nancy — a pickleball player, not tech-savvy, simple by preference. Opens the app on Android before or during gym sessions. Needs zero learning curve. Cal tests on iPhone. No login, no accounts — pure personal tool sent as a link.

### Brand Personality
Warm, feminine, encouraging. Three words: **personal · soft · motivating**. The app should feel like it was built just for her — not generic fitness app, not clinical tracker.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme (gym context, evening use). Palette: deep black `#080808`, amethyst `#A78BFA`, teal `#2DD4BF`, mauve/rose `#C8A4C0`. Playfair Display for headlines (elegant, italic-forward), Nunito for body (friendly, rounded). Pickleball references are intentional and personal — not decoration, her identity. Anti-reference: generic fitness apps (MyFitnessPal, Nike Training), cold neon aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — if Nancy has to think about it, it's too complex
2. **Personal feels designed** — her name, her sport, her colors throughout
3. **Celebrate effort** — every session complete should feel like a win (confetti, streak)
4. **Feminine without being fragile** — soft palette but strong type, not pastel overload
5. **One job per screen** — home picks a workout, workout logs sets, progress shows growth
